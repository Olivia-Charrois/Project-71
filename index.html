CAMPUS LIFE

School Church

was built in 1920

The first Sacre-Coeur was founded in 1920 by Father Rosario Tanguay, who arrived in Welland in October 1919. It was originally a home that sat just north of the current church. A second church was built on the home's foundation in 1934. The current Sacre-Coeur was built in the late '50s and blessed in 1960.
